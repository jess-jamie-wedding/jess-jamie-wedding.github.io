RSVP

Local Area

Getting There

Direct trains from St Pancras/Victoria/London Bridge go to Whitstable/Faversham/Canterbury in 1-1.5hr.

Things to Do

Whitstable is a seaside town with a lovely high street, shingle beaches and an active harbour. Canterbury is a very historical city with a lot of beautiful architecture.

Food & Drink

Our favourites include the Lobster Shack and Tudor Tea Rooms (for ham, egg and chips). Plenty of gastropubs in the area.

Accommodation

Hotels

Top-end hotels include The Pig and Crescent Turner. There is also a Travelodge and Holiday Inn Express within 10 min drive.

Campsites

Camping and glamping options are available at Brook Farm, Rosewood and Chapter Farm.

Airbnbs

Plenty of airbnbs available nearby, or Whitstable (seaside) and Canterbury are each 15 mins away and lovely places to stay.
